Starlight

About Starlight

Genre: Pop

Starlight is a rising pop sensation known for catchy melodies and vibrant performances. With a career spanning just two years, she has already released a platinum-selling debut album and performed at major music festivals around the world.

Early Life

Born in a small town, Starlight began singing in her school choir at age 6. She moved to the city at 18 to pursue her music career, working multiple jobs during the day and performing at local venues at night.

Career Highlights

Her breakthrough single "Shooting Star" topped charts in 15 countries. The music video has over 500 million views on streaming platforms. She has won two music awards and is nominated for two more this year.

Neon Pulse

About Neon Pulse

Genre: Electronic

Neon Pulse is an electronic music duo consisting of synthesizer virtuoso Alex Rivera and drummer/producer Jamie Chen. Their unique blend of retro synthwave and modern electronic beats has earned them a dedicated following worldwide.

Formation

The duo formed in 2019 when Rivera and Chen met at a music technology conference. They bonded over their shared love of 80s synthpop and decided to create something that honored that era while pushing it forward.

Success Story

Their debut album "Digital Horizons" received critical acclaim and reached #3 on the electronic charts. Their live performances feature elaborate LED setups and have become a must-see at electronic music festivals.

Synthwave Duo

About Synthwave Duo

Genre: Synthwave

Synthwave Duo consists of siblings Maya and Kai Thompson who create nostalgic electronic music inspired by 80s aesthetics. Their music evokes the feeling of driving through neon-lit cityscapes at night.

Background

Growing up in the 90s, the Thompson siblings were fascinated by their parents' 80s memorabilia. They began creating music together in their teens, initially as a fun hobby that eventually became their career.

Rise to Fame

Their viral hit "Midnight Drive" garnered over 10 million streams in its first month. They've collaborated with several retro game developers to create soundtracks and have performed at numerous retro-themed events.
