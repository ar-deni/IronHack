About Starlight

Genre: Pop

Starlight is a rising pop sensation known for catchy melodies and vibrant performances. With a career spanning just two years, she has already released a platinum-selling debut album and performed at major music festivals around the world.

Early Life

Born in a small town, Starlight began singing in her school choir at age 6. She moved to the city at 18 to pursue her music career, working multiple jobs during the day and performing at local venues at night.

Career Highlights

Her breakthrough single "Shooting Star" topped charts in 15 countries. The music video has over 500 million views on streaming platforms. She has won two music awards and is nominated for two more this year.
